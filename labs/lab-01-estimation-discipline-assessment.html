📐 Lab 1: Estimation Discipline Assessment

Module 1 — Agile Project Estimation | Duration: 45 minutes | Style: Workshop

🎯 Lab Objectives

  • Assess your current estimation practices against Agile discipline standards
  • Identify gaps between current approaches and Agile estimation principles
  • Experience the difference between people-driven and formula-driven estimation
  • Apply the "Agile Microscope" concept to a sample project scenario

3 People vs Formulas Experiment

15 minutes

In groups of 3-4, perform the following estimation experiment:

Round 1: Individual Silent Estimate

Each person independently writes down how many hours they think it would take to complete these tasks for the customer portal. Do not share your estimates yet.

  1. Build user login and authentication
  2. Create order history display page
  3. Implement support ticket submission form
  4. Build knowledge base search functionality
  5. Create admin dashboard for customer support team

Round 2: Group Discussion Estimate

Now share your estimates with the group. Discuss the differences. As a group, agree on a single estimate for each task.

Round 3: Reflection

  • How wide was the range between individual estimates?
  • What assumptions were different between team members?
  • Did the group discussion surface risks or unknowns?
  • Is the group estimate more accurate than any individual estimate? Why?
