🃏 Lab 4: Planning Poker & Velocity Workshop

Module 4 — Agile Project Estimation | Duration: 45 minutes (30 min work + 15 min presentations) | Style: Group Workshop

🎯 Lab Objectives

  • Practice a Planning Poker estimation session as a Scrum team
  • Calculate team velocity and understand how it creates a forecast range
  • Translate velocity ranges into calendar date forecasts with confidence levels

👥 Group Setup

Form groups of 4-6 people. Assign: Product Owner (reads stories), Scrum Master (facilitates), Developers (estimate).

Tools: Physical cards, fingers, digital tool, or sticky notes — your choice.

Presentation: Each team presents estimates and forecast range (3-5 min per team).

Reference Story (= 3 Story Points)

"As a new hire, I want to fill out and submit a personal information form so that HR has my contact details on file."

Involves: form UI, validation, database storage, confirmation message.
