📦 Lab 3: Story Decomposition & INVEST Workshop

Module 3 — Agile Project Estimation | Duration: 65 minutes (50 min work + 15 min presentations) | Style: Group Workshop

🎯 Lab Objectives

  • Collaborate as a team to decompose epics into themes, features, and user stories
  • Apply the INVEST criteria collectively to evaluate and improve stories
  • Identify spikes as a team — uncertainty is best surfaced through group discussion
  • Build a prioritized product backlog through consensus

👥 Group Setup

Form groups of 4-5 people. Assign roles:

  • Product Owner: Represents business value, prioritizes the backlog
  • Scrum Master: Facilitates discussion, keeps the group focused
  • Developers (remaining members): Provide technical perspective on feasibility and decomposition

Tools & Presentation

Choose your tools: Whiteboard, sticky notes, index cards, spreadsheet, or any combination your team prefers.

Class presentation: Each team presents their decomposition and prioritized backlog (3-5 minutes per team).

4 Team Presentation to Class

15 minutes (3-5 min per team)

Each team presents:

  • Your theme structure and decomposition approach
  • Your best and worst INVEST examples — and how you fixed the failures
  • Your prioritized backlog and any spikes identified

Compare backlogs across teams — how did different groups decompose the same epic?

💡 Discussion Points

  • What was the hardest part of decomposing the epic?
  • Which INVEST criterion is most commonly violated in real projects?
  • How do spikes reduce estimation risk?
  • How would you avoid the "hidden waterfall" where all design is done first, then all development?
